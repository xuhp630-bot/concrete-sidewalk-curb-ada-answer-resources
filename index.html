Sidewalk And Curb Question Sequence

  1. Identify whether the scope is new sidewalk, replacement, repair, curb, gutter, or ADA ramp work.
  2. Check square footage, linear footage, thickness, slope, access, demolition, and permit assumptions.
  3. Separate replacement, repair, and trip-hazard work before comparing bids.
  4. Use quote checklists to clarify cleanup, traffic control, inspections, and finish.
